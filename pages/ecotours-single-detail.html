Charter Overview

Looking for a unique setting for your special occasion? Pacific Whale Foundation is proud to offer exclusive private charters, arranged to meet your needs and style of adventure for groups from 2 to 450 guests. Whether you are getting married, holding a corporate event, or just want to party with your friends, let our luxury vessels and attentive staff help create memories to last a lifetime. We are also offering Shared Private Charters.

Book one of our Tours

Your private charter is staffed with a U.S. Coast Guard certified captain and Certified Marine Naturalist, all of whom hold graduate or undergraduate degrees in marine biology or related sciences and undergo continual professional training on wildlife behaviors and biology, Hawaii’s marine environment, and local geography and culture. All crew members are certified in First Aid, oxygen administration, and CPR for adults, children and infants and are certified lifeguards.

PacWhale is a proud member of the Hawaii Ecotourism Association and is a Certified Sustainable Tour Operator. From following strict wildlife viewing guidelines to using reusable or compostable plates, cups and utensils — we work to ensure that all operations are as environmentally friendly as possible.

Safety Tips

  • Guests need to use hand railings and step carefully when entering and exiting vessel on gangway.
  • No standing up on benches or chairs.
  • No sitting on hand railings, tables, counters or chair backs.
  • No smoking or vaping of any substance.
  • No children on shoulders.
  • Always keep a free hand for a railing or other fixed part of the boat, never carry more than can be taken in one hand. 
  • Stay hydrated. 
  • Wear low or flat-soled shoes.
